Inspirations



When I first set out on my project, I knew that I wanted to create something visually stunning, but I wasn’t quite sure where to start. As I explored different resources and ideas, I found myself deeply inspired by a few standout techniques, videos, and fonts that have truly shaped the design and feel of my project. Here's a look into the tools and inspirations that guided my creative process.

Animated Backgrounds:

Bringing Life to the Design One of the first things that captivated me was the power of animated backgrounds. I stumbled upon a YouTube video titled "Animated Gradient Background with Pure CSS", and it was like a lightbulb moment. The way gradients could smoothly transition and create a dynamic, yet subtle, background effect really resonated with me. It added a level of sophistication and movement to the design that felt modern and fresh. I immediately knew I had to incorporate something similar into my project.

Responsive Design:

Ensuring a Seamless Experience Creating a responsive design was another top priority. I found the video "Master Responsive CSS Media Queries in Easy Way" incredibly insightful. The way it broke down media queries into digestible chunks made it so much easier to ensure my site looked great on any device. The tips from this video were game-changers, allowing me to refine my design for different screen sizes without losing the essence of my original layout.

Texture and Feel: Adding Depth with Grainy Textures and Morphing Gradient

Adding Depth with Grainy Textures While sleek, clean designs are appealing, I also wanted to experiment with adding a bit of texture to the project. That’s when I came across "I Tried Designing and Coding Grainy Textures for a Week". This video opened my eyes to the possibilities of incorporating grainy textures into web design. The subtle, almost imperceptible roughness adds a tactile quality to the UI, making the digital experience feel a little more tangible and engaging.

Typography and Transitions: The Power of Fonts and Loading Animations

The choice of typography can make or break a design. For this project, I wanted fonts that were both unique and versatile. I chose Kola for its bold, modern feel, perfect for headings and titles. Its clean lines and strong presence command attention without overwhelming the viewer. For body text, I went with Averia Serif Libre. This font has a warm, approachable quality that’s easy on the eyes, making it ideal for longer reads. The slight imperfections in its curves give it a handmade, personal touch, aligning perfectly with the human-centric design approach I wanted to achieve. To complement the typography and ensure the site’s performance is just as impressive as its aesthetics, I explored different loading animations. One of my favorites was from a CodePen, featuring a smooth and engaging animation that keeps users entertained while they wait. These small details add polish to the project and ensure a seamless experience from start to finish.

Wrapping Up

This project has been a journey of discovery and creativity. Each of these resources has played a significant role in shaping the final product. From animated backgrounds to responsive designs, grainy textures to morphing gradients, and the perfect fonts to engaging loading animations, these inspirations have helped me craft a user experience that is both visually striking and deeply satisfying to interact with. I’m excited to see how these elements come together as the project continues to evolve.
